# cn-sde-jobhunt — Framings (Layer 2)

Framing library for `cn-sde-jobhunt`. Each framing is a lens — the
way one community or tradition argues about decisions in this
vertical. The six anchor framings below correspond to the six
named in `docs/specs/ROADMAP.md` §3. Each maps to one or more
communities (Layer 0) and applies across multiple decisions in
`decisions.md` (Layer 1).

Entries below are structural-only: per-entry paragraph content is
voice-work, authored by the human only.
